About Us

We are one of the oldest technical societies at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka. Besides organizing technical and cultural events and competitions for students of the institution, we also host a range of educational seminars, events, and annual projects . We strive to promote a passion for technology and liberal arts in our members via Special Interest Groups (SIGs) within the club.
